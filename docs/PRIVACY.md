# Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

## Data Collection

GreasyFork Premium does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data.

No analytics, tracking systems, telemetry, or advertising services are used.

## Local Storage

Some settings may be stored locally in your browser to improve your experience, such as:

* Theme preferences
* Favorites
* Interface settings

This information never leaves your device.

## External Resources

GreasyFork Premium may load third-party resources required for specific features.

At the time of writing, Highlight.js is used for code highlighting.

## Open Source

The entire source code is publicly available on GitHub and can be audited by anyone.

## Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated when necessary. Any significant changes will be documented in future releases.

## Contact

If you have privacy-related questions, please open an issue on GitHub.
