## Step 2 — Install GreasyFork Premium

Open the **[GreasyFork Premium page](https://greasyfork.org/scripts/562938-greasyfork-premium)**.

Click **Install this script**, then confirm the installation in your userscript manager.

## Step 3 — Refresh GreasyFork

After installation, open or refresh:

- <https://greasyfork.org>
- <https://sleazyfork.org>

GreasyFork Premium will be applied automatically.

## Updating

Most userscript managers automatically check for script updates.

You can also manually check for updates from your userscript manager dashboard.
